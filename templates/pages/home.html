Manage Tasks with Roles and Teams

With Favorite Task Manager, you can streamline your task management by assigning various roles to your team members. Each role has specific responsibilities and permissions, ensuring efficient collaboration and accountability.

The roles include:

  • Product Owner: Creates tasks and sets their requirements and priorities.
  • Developer: Assigned to tasks and responsible for implementing and updating them.
  • Scrum Master: Oversees the development process, facilitates communication, and removes obstacles.
  • Manager (Admin): Monitors and manages the overall task management process.

Roles and Permissions

The Favorite Task Manager ensures that the right individuals have the appropriate access and permissions for task management.

Only the Product Owner can create new tasks, while both the Product Owner and the assigned Developer have the authority to modify or delete tasks.

The Scrum Master plays a vital role in the development process by facilitating collaboration, resolving issues, and ensuring smooth workflow. They do not have direct access to task creation or modification but contribute to overall team success.

The Manager (Admin) role has access to a comprehensive report page, allowing them to view and analyze all issues across different teams. This enables effective monitoring and decision-making at a higher level.
